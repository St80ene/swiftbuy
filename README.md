# 🚀 SwiftBuy

Never run out of stock again.

**SwiftBuy** is a lightweight inventory and sales management platform built for small and medium-sized businesses (SMEs). It helps business owners track products, monitor stock levels, record sales, and receive low-stock alerts before inventory runs out.

---

## 🎯 Vision

Many businesses still manage inventory using notebooks, spreadsheets, or WhatsApp messages. **SwiftBuy** aims to simplify inventory operations by providing a central system for tracking stock movements and sales in real time.

---

## ✨ MVP Features

### Product Management
* Create and manage products
* Organize products by category
* Define cost price and selling price
* Set low-stock thresholds

### Inventory Management
* Add stock
* Remove stock
* Adjust stock levels
* Track inventory movement history

### Sales Tracking
* Record sales transactions
* Automatically deduct sold quantities from inventory
* Monitor daily sales performance

### Dashboard
* Total products
* Current inventory value
* Today's sales
* Low-stock products

### Low Stock Alerts
* Receive notifications when product quantities fall below a predefined threshold.
